About The Project

Our project revolves around a volunteering management system that can be used by both the event organizers and volunteers. Our project is website based and is tailored for volunteers who wish to help out their community as well as to organizers, who are seeking volunteers for their organizations. This project is a rating-based system in which the organizers and volunteers can rate their experience and provide a short review in order to improve the quality of the events being posted and help future volunteers and organizers. The volunteers will be able to explore the list of ongoing events, filter them to their desired parameters, enlist in events, subscribe to mailing lists to stay updated, and rate the organizer. The organizers will be responsible for posting events, sending announcements, and rating the volunteers.
